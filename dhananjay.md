## 📂 Selected Projects 🚀

### **🌊 SmartFlow** — Data Quality & Validation Pipeline
**Tech:** `SQL Server`, `FastAPI`, `Scikit-Learn`, `Gemini API`
*   Solves **"Garbage In, Garbage Out"** using Isolation Forests + LLM parsing.
*   Flags and quarantines anomalous records before downstream usage.
*   Designed as a **production backend service** for real-world data pipelines. 🛡️

### **🛡️ CRIS** — Customer Risk Intelligence System
**Tech:** `SQL Server`, `SQLAlchemy`, `Scikit-Learn`, `Docker`
*   Predicts churn and credit risk using **SQL-first feature aggregation**.
*   High-performance feature store with ML-driven risk scorecards. 📊

### **🧠 MetricMind** — Predictive Analytics Platform
**Tech:** `Docker`, `Scikit-Learn`, `Streamlit`, `Pytest`
*   End-to-end **sales forecasting system** with a "What-If" simulator.
*   Built with **Test-Driven Development (TDD)** and containerized for deployment. 📦

### **⛏️ DocuMinr** — LLM-Based Document Intelligence
**Tech:** `LangChain`, `Google Gemini`, `FAISS`, `Docker`
*   Converts unstructured PDFs into **strictly validated JSON outputs**.
*   Schema-first design to **reduce LLM hallucinations** and enable SQL export. 📄
